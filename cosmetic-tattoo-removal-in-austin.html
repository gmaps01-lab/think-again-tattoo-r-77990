How many sessions will I need to fully remove my cosmetic tattoo?
The number of sessions varies based on factors like pigment color, depth, skin type, and age of the tattoo. Most cosmetic tattoos, such as microbladed brows, require between 2 to 6 sessions spaced 6-8 weeks apart. What is the process for removing cosmetic tattoos like eyebrows or lip liner?
At our Austin clinic, we use medical-grade lasers specifically calibrated for cosmetic tattoo pigments.

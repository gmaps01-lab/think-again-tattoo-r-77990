How much does tattoo removal typically cost at Think Again Tattoo Removal in Austin?

The number of sessions needed varies based on factors like tattoo size, ink colors, skin type, and how your body responds to laser treatments. What is the tattoo removal process like, and what should I expect after each session?
We use advanced laser technology that targets the ink particles in your skin, breaking them down so your body can naturally remove them. Each session lasts 15-30 minutes depending on the tattoo’s size. The process can cause some discomfort, similar to a rubber band snap, but we offer cooling devices and numbing agents for your comfort. After each session, you may experience redness, swelling, or blistering—these are normal and usually subside within a week. We’ll provide detailed aftercare instructions and are always available for follow-up questions.
Do you offer any guarantees if my tattoo isn’t completely removed?
While we strive for the best possible results, complete removal can’t always be guaranteed due to factors like ink depth, colors, and individual skin response. At Think Again Tattoo Removal, we believe in transparency and will discuss your expected outcome during your consultation.
